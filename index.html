How to Play Linkle

Deduce the secret URL in 6 tries. Select components from the terminals below to build your guess.

Feedback Codes

  • A 200 OK (Green) tile means the component is correct and in the right place.
  • A 200 OK - Wrong Host (Blue) tile means the Subdomain, Domain, and TLD you guessed form a REAL, live website, but it's not the secret one. This is a huge clue!
  • A 404 Not Found (Gray) tile means the component is not in the secret URL.

Ping the network. Find the link. Good luck!
