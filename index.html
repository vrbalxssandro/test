Color Code

How to Play

Your goal is to guess the secret 4-color code.

The 4 colors in the code are unique and selected from the 6 colors in the palette.

You have 6 attempts to guess the code.

Feedback Pegs

White Peg: Correct color in the correct position.

Gray Peg: Correct color in the wrong position.
